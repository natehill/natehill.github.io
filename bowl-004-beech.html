Hello!

I'm Nate Hill.
This page features wooden bowls and other items that I make, most of which are for sale. If you are interested in my library and cultural heritage work, you can get into that over here.
All of these bowls are made from wood that I find locally or driving around my region in upstate New York. I’m particularly interested in spalted wood, wood that has begun to decay. The patterns, textures, and tones that fungi and other agents bring to the wood give it unique character. In the future, I‘ll be adding sculptural wooden boxes and other fixtures to the page as well.
Thanks for looking, and please reach out with any questions to nathanielhill@gmail.com or find me on Instagram as @natehillnatehill.

Small Spalted Beech Bowl

Height: 2.5"

Width: 7.5"

I love this bowl. My neighbor lost a massive American Beech tree in a storm almost 3 years ago, and I keep on bringing the saw over to the woods behind his house and lopping off pieces of it to play with as it spalts and ages. This is a small bowl, but it has so much character. The grain looks like fire, or a filet of fish. It has a lovely, warm, natural warp to it.
