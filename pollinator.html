You're the Pollinator, 2024

You’re the Pollinator offers an interpretation of a saying sometimes attributed to the scupltor Richard Serra, “If you’re not paying for the product, you are the product.” Turning the switch on the central lower armature activates the lights, inviting the viewer to perfrom a service that benefits the sculpture: with the lights on, the sculpture become much more visibly dramatic by casting shadows.

This piece is carved walnut and stands about 7.5 ft tall. The inspiration came from the forms and habits of orchids, drones, poppies, and skeletal structures. The flexible components have gooseneck armatures beneath the walnut rings, so it can be positioned in a variety of ways.
